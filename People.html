Meet the research team.

James Kryklywy

Principal Investigator

Originally from Northern Ontario, James obtained his B.Sc. (Psychology & Physiology, 2009) and Ph.D. (Neuroscience, 2016) from the University of Western Ontario, before completing postdoctoral training in cognitive neuroscience at athe University of British Columbia. James is currently an Assistant Professor in Psychology at Lakehead University.

Oluwaseun Daniel Olusanya

MSc Candidate, Psych Science

Daniel is entering his first year as a Masters of Psychological Sciences student at Lakehead University. He hopes to contribute to the literature on Callous Unemotional traits by studying how people high in CU traits respond to sensory information (tactile, auditory, and visual). Outside the realm of education, Daniel enjoys two things Television shows and sports (his favourite sports teams are Arsenal F.C and the Dallas Mavericks).
